About SAJILOSATHI

I started the SAJILOSATHI project as a learning opportunity and to address problems within child rights. I am Apil Bhandari, a student from Motherland Secondary School and the treasurer at Mcf (Metropolitan Child Forum). You can visit my portfolio here, or submmit any complaints at complaints/feedback page.

"Child rights are fundamental, yet they face numerous challenges globally. SAJILOSATHI acknowledges the gravity of these issues and aims to be a part of the solution. Through this project, we provide essential local government contact numbers, specifically targeted at cases concerning child labor in regions like Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, and Lalitpur. Moreover, SAJILOSATHI offers a platform where individuals can voice concerns and file complaints via complaints.my.to. This platform is vital in addressing child rights violations and advocating for better protection of our children. By collectively participating in initiatives like SAJILOSATHI, we contribute to building a world where every child's rights are respected, protected, and upheld. Together, we strive towards creating a brighter and safer future for our children."
